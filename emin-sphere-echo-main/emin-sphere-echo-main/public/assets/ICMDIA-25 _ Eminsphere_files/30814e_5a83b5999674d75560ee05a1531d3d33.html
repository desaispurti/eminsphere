Official Launch of the ICMDIA-25 Conference Proceedings

The International Conference on Multidisciplinary Developments in Innovation and Advancement (ICMDIA-25), held on December 14, 2025 in Hybrid Mode, proudly announces the release of its official conference proceedings. This volume presents selected research papers, keynote contributions, and multidisciplinary innovations addressing contemporary challenges and future directions in science, technology, and society.
