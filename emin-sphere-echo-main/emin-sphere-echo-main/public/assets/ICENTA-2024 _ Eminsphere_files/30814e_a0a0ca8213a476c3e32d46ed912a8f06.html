Conference Overview

ICENTA-2024 is a global initiative by Eminsphere to foster innovation, collaboration, and knowledge exchange in Engineering, AI, IoT, Robotics, and Sustainable Technologies. Renowned academicians, researchers, industry experts, and policymakers will discuss the future of engineering advancements.

Objectives

Global Collaboration

Strengthen international research collaborations and engineering innovations.

Cutting-Edge Research

Present breakthroughs in AI, IoT, Industry 4.0, and automation.

Future Trends

Explore the intersection of engineering, sustainability, and smart technology.

Industrial-Academic Synergy

Bridge the gap between academia and industry for real-world applications.

Policy Discussions

Engage policymakers to drive technology-forward policies for global benefit.
